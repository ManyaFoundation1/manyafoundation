❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Still thinking? Let us help you feel sure.

❓Where does my donation go?

Your donation goes directly to feed cows, provide ration kits to poor families, or give stationery kits to needy children — depending on what you choose.

❓Will I get proof?

✅ Yes! We send real photos or videos of the seva done in your name via WhatsApp within 1–3 days.

❓Can I choose what I want to donate for?

Yes! You can pick: • ₹50 to feed a cow 🐄 • ₹100 to support a child’s education ✏️ • ₹500+ to give a ration kit 🍚 You can also WhatsApp us if you want to do something specific.

❓Is this tax-exempt under 80G?

Yes, we are a registered NGO. You will get an 80G donation receipt to claim tax exemption in India.

❓Is this genuine?

We’ve already served over 17,000+ lives across cows, families, and kids — all with transparent proof. 💬 You can even message our team before donating.

❓What if I have a special request?

No problem. After donating, just send us a WhatsApp message. We try our best to honour every seva wish — be it in memory of someone, on a birthday, or for a cause close to your heart. 🙏

❓Do you only feed in one city?

We currently operate in multiple areas and continue to expand with your support.
